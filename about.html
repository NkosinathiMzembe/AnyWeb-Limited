WHAT TO KNOW ABOUT BAUXITE INTERNATIONAL

As We Find Learning To Be A Fun Experience

. . .

The institution aims at instilling a holistic education into students while developing them into becoming responsible adults; well vest in knowledge, ready to improve and transform the African continent.

The institution consists of Pre-school, Primary School and Secondary School in which students are trained to be critical and analytical thinkers who can identify and solve real-world problems.

The school is a non-profit institution as it sees the relevance in investing in education and all resources that come along with it. Seeking to render their services to both African children and parents as well as those in the diaspora.

Our history

Bauxite International School (BIS) is an institution located in Accra-East Legon, Ghana which was established in 2005 by Dr J. Dari with the mission to educate young children in Africa and the diaspora.

Over the years, the school has been able to bridge the gap in quality of education
