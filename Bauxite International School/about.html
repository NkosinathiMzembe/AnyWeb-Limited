About us

This institution aims at instilling a holistic education into students while developing them into becoming responsible adults; well vest in knowledge, ready to improve and transform the African continent.
Students are trained to be critical and analytical thinkers who can identify and solve real-world problems.
The institution consists of Pre-school, Primary School and Secondary School. The school is a non-profit institution as it sees the relevance in investing in education and all resources that come along with it. Seeking to render their services to both African children and parents as well as those in the diaspora. For the school to easily manage its operations, a web-based application that fosters the following operations, as well as structure, would enable the accessibility to different services to be efficient and beneficial to both the institution as well as all stakeholders involved.

Our history

Bauxite International School (BIS) is an institution located in Accra-East Legon, Ghana which was established in 2005 by Dr J. Dari with the mission of educating young children in Africa and the diaspora. Over the years, the school has been able to bridge the gap in quality of education.
